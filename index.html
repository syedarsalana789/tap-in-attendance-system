TAP IN

No More Proxy, Just One Tap

Introduction

Tap In is a smart attendance management system designed for faculty and staff. It uses biometric technologies including fingerprint and facial recognition to ensure secure and accurate attendance tracking. The system records in/out timings, manages leave applications, and provides real-time reports and analytics.

Problem Statement

Traditional attendance systems are manual, error-prone, and allow proxy attendance. Managing attendance, shifts, and leave records manually leads to inefficiency, lack of transparency, and time wastage.

Attendance is marked using biometric devices. Data is transmitted to the server and stored in a centralized database. The system processes attendance records and automatically detects late arrivals and early departures based on predefined shift timings.

System Roles

User Panel
  • View attendance
  • Apply for leaves
  • Check late and early status
Admin Panel
  • Manage staff and roles
  • Manage shifts and holidays
  • Approve or reject leave requests
Department Panel
  • Manage department users
  • View attendance records
  • Approve leave applications

Key Features

  • Biometric Attendance (Fingerprint & Face Recognition)
  • Automatic Late and Early Detection
  • Leave Management System
  • Role-Based Dashboards
  • Holiday and Shift Management
  • Real-Time Data Synchronization
