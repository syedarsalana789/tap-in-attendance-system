TAP IN

No More Proxy, Just One Tap

Introduction

Tap In is a centralized attendance system using fingerprint and face recognition. It ensures secure tracking of attendance, leaves, and working hours.

Problem

Manual systems are error-prone and allow proxy attendance. Managing shifts and records is inefficient.
