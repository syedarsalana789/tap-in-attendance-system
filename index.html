About the System

Introduction

Tap In is a smart attendance management system built for university faculty and staff. It uses biometric technologies — fingerprint scanning and facial recognition — to ensure secure, accurate, and tamper-proof attendance tracking. The system records in/out timings, manages leave applications, and delivers real-time analytics through role-based dashboards.

The Challenge

Problem Statement

Traditional attendance systems are manual, error-prone, and vulnerable to proxy attendance. Managing records, shifts, and leaves manually creates inefficiency, a lack of transparency, and significant time wastage for both staff and administration.

Dashboard

Attendance is marked via biometric devices and transmitted to a centralized server. Records are stored in a database, then processed to auto-detect late arrivals and early departures based on predefined shift timings — displayed live across all role dashboards.

Access Control

System Roles

User Panel

  • View personal attendance records
  • Apply for leave requests
  • Check late & early departure status

Admin Panel

  • Manage all staff and roles
  • Configure shifts & holidays
  • Approve or reject leave requests
  • View system-wide analytics

Department Panel

  • Manage department-level users
  • View department attendance records
  • Approve leave applications
