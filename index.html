TAP IN

Introduction

Tap In is a smart attendance management system designed for faculty and staff using biometric fingerprint and facial recognition. It ensures secure attendance tracking, automated reporting, and efficient leave management.

System Roles

User

View attendance, apply leaves, check records

Admin

Manage users, shifts, leaves, and reports

Department

Manage department staff and approvals
