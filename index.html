TAP IN

No More Proxy, Just One Tap

Introduction

Tap In is a centralized attendance management system designed for faculty and staff. It uses biometric technologies including fingerprint and facial recognition to ensure secure and accurate attendance tracking. It records in/out timings and manages attendance, leaves, and reports.

Problem Statement

Traditional attendance systems are manual and error-prone, leading to proxy attendance and inefficiency. Tap In eliminates these issues by providing automation, transparency, and accuracy.

Smart Methodology

Users mark attendance via thumb or face recognition. Data is automatically sent to the server and stored in the database. The system detects late arrivals and early departures based on predefined shift timings set by admin.

Key Features

  • Biometric Attendance (Fingerprint & Face)
  • Automatic Late & Early Detection
  • Leave Management (Sick & Annual)
  • User Dashboard (View attendance & apply leave)
  • Admin Dashboard (Manage staff, shifts & leaves)
  • Department Dashboard (Manage specific faculty)
  • Holiday Management (Eid, Labour Day, Breaks)
  • Role-based Access Control
  • Real-time Data Sync from Device
  • Centralized Database (MySQL)

Scope & Application

This system is suitable for universities and organizations. It enables real-time tracking, efficient attendance monitoring, and better staff management through automation.
