ABOUT PROJECT

Smart Attendance Solution

Tap In provides a secure and intelligent way to manage attendance records with automation and transparency.

Introduction

Tap In is a smart attendance management system designed for faculty and staff. It uses fingerprint and facial recognition technology to ensure secure and accurate attendance tracking with real-time reporting and leave management.

Problem Statement

Traditional attendance systems are manual and error-prone. Proxy attendance, inefficient leave handling, and poor transparency create major management problems for institutions and organizations.
