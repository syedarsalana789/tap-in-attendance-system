Project Overview

Tap In transforms traditional attendance systems into a smart automated biometric solution.

Introduction

Tap In is a smart attendance management system designed for faculty and staff. It uses biometric technologies including fingerprint and facial recognition to ensure secure and accurate attendance tracking.

Problem Statement

Traditional attendance systems are manual, error-prone, and allow proxy attendance. Managing shifts and leave records manually causes inefficiency, lack of transparency, and time wastage.

System Methodology

Smart workflow architecture for attendance tracking and data management.
